Chilly Chicken Recipe

Jun 27,2018

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 30mins
  • Yields: 4 Servings

01.

Heat enough oil in a pan for frying chicken pieces. Meanwhile take chicken and add corn flour, maida, soya sauce, salt, Black pepper and semolina. Mix well and fry in hot oil one by one until golden brown.

02.

Take a pan, heat some oil and then add ginger-garlic and chopped green chillies let it saute for 2 mins. Then add onions and saute for another 2 mins.

03.

Then add capsicum let it stir fry for another 2 mins. Meanwhile in a bowl take some water add corn flour to it, mix well and then add soya sauce, vinegar, chicken sauce, and salt

04.

Take this mixture of sauces and transfer to the pan, let it come to a boil and then add chicken pieces mix well.
